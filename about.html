Meet Neenu

With a whisk in one hand and a passion in her heart, Neenu transformed her childhood love for baking into Neenus Cakery. What began as simple home recipes has blossomed into a celebration of flavor and artistry.

Every creation reflects her belief that desserts should be both visually stunning and soul-satisfying. When not in the kitchen, you'll find Neenu exploring local markets for inspiration and the freshest ingredients.
